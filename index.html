About Us

We are a high-energy dance collective specializing in hip-hop, contemporary fusion, and street styles. Our mission is to inspire, create, and perform with unmatched intensity. At WEST CULTURE DANCE UNIT, donations are at the heart of what we do. We don't just collect support—we actively distribute it to create lasting change in our communities here in Kampala, Central Region, and surrounding areas.What we do through donations: Provide food packages and groceries to vulnerable families and elderly people Supply school materials, uniforms, and fees to help children stay in education Offer clothing, blankets, and hygiene items during tough seasons Support medical needs, like medicines or hospital contributions for the sick Assist orphans, single mothers, and low-income households with emergency aid We carry out these donations regularly—monthly drives, special holiday distributions, and urgent responses when crises arise. Everything is done transparently: we share photos/videos of deliveries (with privacy respected), receipts where possible, and stories of real impact. Your support matters! Whether it's: Cash contributions (via mobile money, bank, etc.) In-kind donations (food, clothes, books, etc.) Volunteering time to help pack/distribute Spreading the word to friends/family ...every bit helps someone smile, eat, learn, or heal.
